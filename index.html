Welcome To Oasis

The King’s Village Oasis of Love Orphanage is a mercy project and an outreach complex built, operated and managed by Jesus is Lord Project Mercy Ministry [JILPMM].

The King’s Village Oasis of Love Orphanage is a mercy project and an outreach complex built, operated and managed by Jesus is Lord Project Mercy Ministry [JILPMM]. The vision and mandate of The King’s Village Oasis of Love Orphanage was born out of personal relationship with The Lord Jesus Christ. The establishment and operation of the orphanage is a practical product of hearing and obeying the voice (word) of The Lord Jesus Christ.
