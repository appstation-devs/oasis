What We Do?

Generally, Our Vision is to take care of Orphans by creating an enabling environment for the growth and wellbeing of the children, with trained caregivers nurturing the children.

Providing Decent Homes

To Provide decent homes in Family setting so as to instil in the children Family and Godly Values

Providing Quality Education

To Provide Pre-Nursery, Nursery, Primary and Secondary Education based on Godly principles

Providing Life Skills

To Equip Our Children with Skills to Prepare Them For Life

Our Policies

Policies of the king’s village oasis of love orphanage – a mercy project of Jesus is Lord Project Mercy Ministry. It is the policy of the administration of this orphanage to provide the following for every child that is fostered in this home:

  • Raise and train a child from primary to tertiary education as we are enabled
  • Raise and train a child to acquire skills to equip the child for life
  • Allow relatives to visit his/her ward when necessary
  • Allow relatives to withdraw his/her ward from the home if they so desire
  • Raise the child in the christian faith and provide parental nurture and care
  • A child can be sent back to relatives if after several counseling sessions, the child refuses to adapt to the rules of the home and constitutes a bad example to other children
  • This orphanage does not have an adoption policy programme
